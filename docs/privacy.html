Minecraft Forge - Privacy Settings

Minecraft Forge uses Google AdSense to display advertisements and make profit.

In order to present only the best and most relevant to you, Curse (and thus, Forge) needs to use cookies. Apart from that, this site uses HTML5 local storage in order to allow for client siding switching between a light and dark theme.

EU law requires us to ask anyone living in the European Union for your consent to this usage of your data.

We ask you to accept this usage, as advertising is the sole source of income for the Forge project. If you do not want this site to handle that data or if you otherwise prevent ads from being displayed, please consider donating to LexManos, using one of the following channels:
